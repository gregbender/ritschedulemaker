Color Printing
If you are having problems printing the schedule in color, try the following

1.

In your Internet Explorer window, choose 'Tools' from the menu bar and click 'Internet Options'.
2.

When the options menu comes up, click the 'Advanced' tab.
3.

Scroll through the list of options, and check the box that says "Print background colors and images".
